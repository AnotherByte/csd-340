Chris Beatty

Hobbies

Sports Car Racing

Chris enjoys watching sports car racing on tv. He frequently goes to Road America, the major race track near his home. Getting to stand right next to the car moments before the race is one of his favorite parts.

Computer Building

Building his computers and upgrading their parts over time is another one of his favorite hobbies. He enjoys choosing what parts are used and comparing them to each other. There is also a visual element with the clean layout and lights.

Video Games

With that computer he built, he spends his free time playing video games. He doesn't have as much free time as he used to so he focuses on two main games. They are a racing game, Forza Horizon, and an online shooter, Destiny 2.

Work Experience

Machine Operator

At Alta Resources, Chris ran a packaging machine that automated the packaging of movies into envelopes for shipping through the mail. At times he would run and maintain the machine while feeding the product himself, and other times would run the machine while managing a small team that would feed the movies.

Backroom

Soon after starting at Target, Chris was a part of the backroom team. They would backstock product that came in from the semi truck that day but would not fit on the floor. Backstocking the product requires physically placing the items in organized bins while also scanning the items into the location on a device to keep inventory accounted for and so it can be easily found when it is needed later.

Receiving

For the last three and a half years, Chris has worked in the receiving position at Target. As the only member of this team, he is responsible for the product that 3rd party vendors bring into the store. Items such as snacks, beverages, and alcoholic beverages are the main types of product he oversees. He is responsible for not only checking in the vendors to make sure the numbers and counts are correct but to take care of the paperwork and keep it all organized.
